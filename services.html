frequently asked questions

How do I book a session?

You can book a session by contacting us through our website, calling our studio, or sending us an email. We will work with you to schedule a time that is convenient for you.

Can I request changes after receiving my photos?

Yes, we offer a round of revisions to ensure you are completely satisfied with your images. Additional revisions may incur an extra fee and some extra charges.

What should I wear for my photoshoot?

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing that reflects your personal style. Solid colors and minimal patterns work best for most shoots. Feel free to bring multiple outfits for variety.

What happens if the weather is bad on the day of an outdoor shoot?

We closely monitor weather conditions and will discuss contingency plans with you. We can reschedule the session or find an alternative indoor location if necessary.

How long does it take to receive my photos?

The turnaround time for receiving your photos varies depending on the type of session. Typically, you can expect to receive your edited images within 2-3 weeks or sometimes more

How will I receive my photos?

You will receive your photos through a secure online gallery where you can view, download, and share your images. We also offer prints and photo books upon request.

Do you offer prints and photo albums?

Yes, we offer a range of print options and custom photo albums. You can select your favorite images and we will create beautiful prints and albums and many more for you to cherish.

Do you offer photography workshops or classes?

Yes, we periodically offer workshops and one-on-one classes covering various aspects of photography. Check our website for upcoming sessions and availability.
